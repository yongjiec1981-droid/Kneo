Strategic Insights

Data-driven conclusions to guide product development and go-to-market strategy

Insight 01

Comfort Is the Universal Pain Point

60% of respondents cite "uncomfortable/hot to wear" as their top dissatisfaction with existing braces. This validates KNEO's core textile-first approach — users are actively seeking a solution they can wear for hours without irritation.

Insight 02

Guidance Gap Creates Adhesion Crisis

50% lack professional rehab guidance at home, and 75% are willing to use an APP for tracking. KNEO's closed-loop ecosystem (hardware + data + plans) directly addresses a service void, not just a product gap.

Insight 03

Pricing Window Is Validated

60% accept ¥200-400 pricing, with 75% willing to pay 20-100% premium over traditional braces. Post-surgical patients show highest tolerance, supporting a tiered pricing strategy (Standard / Pro / Pro+).

Insight 04

Go-to-Market Channels Are Clear

E-commerce (45%) and brand direct channels (30%) dominate preferences. The 25% crowdfunding interest validates a Kickstarter/Indiegogo pilot launch strategy to build early community before retail expansion.
